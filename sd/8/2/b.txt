# SD8.2 Contributing Snakemake Workflows

## Requirements

* [[skill-tree:sd:8:1:b]]
* [[skill-tree:sd:3:1:b]]

## Learning Objectives 

* Creating a Continous Integration Workflow on GitHub: Being able to copy and adapt the CI workflow of the Snakemake Catalog Template to ensure automated testing, linting and formatting control. All to ensure reliable build and release cycles.
* Modularizing existing Workflow Code: Being able to modularize a workflow into semantic units to ensure better maintainability and sub-component testing.
